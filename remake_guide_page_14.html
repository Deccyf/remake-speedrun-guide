Lisa Spawn Manipulation

Lisa will spawn at whatever route you first go down. If you're concerned about her, go the wrong route first and she will be there, then go the other route. Do this again after the box puzzle for a Lisa-free experience.

Lisa Hallway Navigation

  1. Proceed through Lisa Hallway
  2. Run straight through Lisa on the inside of the turn or bait and run down the alternate route
  3. Run towards the box room at the end before the steps going down

Box Room Puzzle

  1. Push the box once to the left
  2. Push 8 times up
  3. Activate the lift

Compactor Section

  1. Back to the elevator (Barry disappears)
  2. Go down the ladder
  3. Push the box 9 times to the left
  4. Push into the compactor
  5. Activate the compactor
  6. Grab the Broken Flamethrower

Lisa Switch and Flamethrower

  1. Back through caves to Lisa's house
  2. Flip the switch next to Lisa
  3. Place the Broken Flamethrower
  4. Leave

Jewellery Box Collection

  1. Wade through flooded hall (ignore snakes)
  2. Navigate to Lisa's Jewellery Box
  3. Open jewellery box to reveal stone ring
  4. Leave up the ladder
What's Next

Section 15 covers the Laboratory section including computer access, fuel management, and preparing for the Tyrant fight. The lab requires both Stone and Metal Object's for access.

Return to Mansion and Laboratory Access

Back through Lisa's Hut

  • Leave the caves back into Lisa's Hut and exit to the Mansion.
  • Head back through the storage room and grab the Stone and Metal Object from the Courtyard entrance.
  • Head to the study door in the Mansion and use the Emblem Key.
  • Inside the study, turn the lamp on and take the Metal Object.

Prepare the Gate Emblem

  • Return to the Main Hall of the mansion.
  • Combine the Stone Ring from the jewellery box with the Metal Object to form the complete emblem.

Access the Laboratory

  • Use the both completed emblems on the gate mechanism.
  • Proceed through the gate to the path leading to the your final encounter with Lisa.

Lisa Encounter on the Lab Approach

After unlocking the Courtyard gate, you'll have one more encounter with Lisa Trevor on the way to the Laboratory.

Lead up to the encounter

  • Carry on down the linear path towards the Lisa's mothers stone tomb.
  • Activate the cutscene with Barry.
  • DO NOT GIVE BARRY HIS GUN BACK! - This is key for labs and the Tyrant fight later.
  • Push the stones off the edge to avoid Lisa.

Safety Notes

  • There is a inventory box and a typewriter here to save and prepare for the final encounter with Lisa.
  • Do not waste ammo here‚Äîpushing the the stones off the edge is safer than fighting her.
