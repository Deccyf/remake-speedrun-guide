Section Objectives

  • Navigate Room 002 with new zombie spawn
  • Complete insecticide puzzle to clear bee hallway
  • Solve book puzzle in Room 003
  • Defeat Plant 42 boss with optimal strategy
  • Obtain Helmet Key and exit Residence

Plant 42 is one of the most consistent boss fights in the speedrun when executed properly. This section also includes the complex book puzzle that must be solved before accessing the boss.

Room 002 Zombie Management

After returning from the shark room, Room 002 now has a zombie spawn that must be managed carefully.

Optimal Zombie Strategy

  1. Run immediately toward the zombie as you enter
  2. Bait a lunge from the zombie
  3. Run into the zombie, pushing it backward
  4. Hopefully you can move around it on the left side, if not, tank the bite or use a defensive item.
Push Technique

Running into zombies during their recovery animation allows you to run through them. The key is maintaining forward momentum during the baiting technique.

Insecticide Puzzle

The gallery hallway is now infested with bees that will damage you. The insecticide puzzle clears this hazard.

Gallery Key Usage

  1. Use the Gallery Key on the locked door
  2. Run to the body on the floor
  3. Grab the Insect Spray
  4. Return to the hallway
  5. Grab the map from the wall (use cancel button to exit immediately and skip text)
  6. Use the Insect Spray on the hole in the wall
Map Interaction Warning

If you accidentally spam buttons on the map, you run the risk of activating the bees coming into the hallway. This triggers a cutscene and spawns bees that you'll need to dodge. Time loss is minimal but avoidable with careful button presses.

Room 003 Access

  1. Return to the gallery after using the insect spray
  2. Grab the 003 Key at the end of the hall
  3. Proceed into Room 003

Book Puzzle Solution

Room 003 contains a complex book puzzle that must be solved to access Plant 42.

Puzzle Setup

  1. Go to the shelf and grab the white book
  2. Open your menu
  3. Equip the Grenade Launcher in first slot (should have 3 incendiary shells loaded)
  4. Use the Red Book in your inventory
Book Puzzle Solution

Move books in this exact order:

1↔4 - 2↔6 - 3↔7

Important: For books 2 and 3, it's faster to move left and grab books 6 and 7 first, then swap them back to their positions.

Puzzle Optimisation

The counter-intuitive book order (grabbing 6 and 7 before placing 2 and 3) saves cursor movement time. Practise this sequence until it becomes muscle memory.

Plant 42 Boss Fight

Proceed through the door to Plant 42. This boss can be defeated quickly with precise execution and proper positioning.

Optimal Strategy

  1. Run up the stairs to the raised platform
  2. Aim at Plant 42
  3. Fire twice with pump cancelling, if you run out of incendiary shells, use grenade rounds after.
  4. This triggers the midway cutscene
  5. Immediately aim up again and quickly fire the third shot
  6. Kill Plant 42 before it closes

RNG Management

If killed fast enough, the closing and reopening animation during the Plant 42 death cutscene can be skipped, saving time.

Best outcome: Plant 42 dies before closing, death cutscene plays immediately

Slow outcome: If Plant 42 is not killed before closing, it will start to spray poison that must be avoided before it will reopen. This adds 3-5 seconds.

Poison Spray

If you're too slow on the third shot, Plant 42 will close and begin charging a poison spray. Move to the sides of the staircase to avoid the spray, wait for it to reopen, then finish it with one more incendiary shell.

Item Collection

  1. After Plant 42 is defeated, un-equip the Grenade Launcher
  2. Grab the Helmet Key from the room
  3. Leave through the double doors
Helmet Key Acquired

The Helmet Key is essential for accessing underground areas in Mansion 2. This key opens the crusher puzzle room and provides access to the elevator leading to the caves.

Residence Exit

Leave the Residence through the exit path, proceeding back to the Mansion.

Snake Encounters on Return

  1. Upon return to the poolside path, several snakes will jump out
  2. They can be avoided with a specific running line (better seen than described)
  3. Snake bites have a chance to poison
Poison Risk

If you are concerned about poison, pick up a Blue Herb from the Residence before you leave. There should be a couple close to the exit. However, inventory space is tight, so only grab if necessary.

Dog Encounter

Running by the dog in the next room, there is a chance for it to hit or grab you as it is now closer than before. Run straight through without stopping to minimise damage risk.

What's Next

Section 11 covers Mansion 2 with Hunter navigation, the crusher puzzle, underground access, and preparing for Yawn 2. Hunters are the most dangerous regular enemies in the game and can one-shot you with their decapitation attack.

Practice Tips

Book Puzzle: Write down the sequence or keep a reference handy until memorised. The order is: 1↔4, 2↔6, 3↔7, 1.

Plant 42 Positioning: The exact positioning (4 steps in, hard left, 5 steps up) is critical. Practise this on dedicated runs to develop muscle memory for the staircase positioning.

Pump Cancelling: This boss requires two pump cancels in quick succession. The timing is: Fire → Release aim → Press forward immediately. Master this technique as it's used on multiple bosses.
