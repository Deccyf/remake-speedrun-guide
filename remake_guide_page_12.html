Yawn 2 Boss Fight

This is the second encounter with Yawn, and unlike the first fight, you must actually defeat the boss this time. The fight requires precise positioning and optimal ammunition usage.

Combat Strategy

  1. Equip the Grenade Launcher with the acid rounds you collected earlier
  2. Position yourself at medium range from Yawn
  3. Fire 4-5 grenade rounds in rapid succession
  4. Use pump cancelling between shots for optimal speed
AMMUNITION MANAGEMENT

Make sure you have at least 5 grenade rounds loaded before starting this fight. Yawn 2 has more health than Yawn 1 and requires sustained firepower to defeat.

Last Book 2 Collection

After defeating Yawn 2, there's an important cutscene where Yawn hits the bookshelf before dying. This cutscene reveals a crucial item that must be collected.

Collection Process

  1. Wait for Yawn's death cutscene to complete
  2. After the cutscene, approach the damaged bookshelf
  3. Look behind the bookshelf for Last Book 2
  4. Collect the book immediately - it's essential for progression
CRITICAL ITEM

Last Book 2 is essential for the speedrun. This book is required for later puzzle solutions and cannot be skipped. Make sure to collect it immediately after the Yawn 2 fight before proceeding to the light puzzle.

Trophy Room Sequence

After defeating Yawn 2, you'll need to solve a gem puzzle in the Trophy Room to access the Red Gem. Run back through murder hall to the east wing and use the key on the room on the top of the staircase.

Puzzle Solution

  1. Turn the light off using the switch next to the door
  2. Move the chest of drawers to underneath the Moose head with the red eyes.
  3. There is an Eagle with a blue eye tracing your movements. Move around the room to the opposite side making sure for the Eagle to follow you.
  4. Run underneath the Eagle to break line of sight and get on the chest of drawers underneath the Moose head and remove the red gem.
  5. Leave the room through the door.
TIMING IS CRITICAL

If the Eagle spots you before you get the red gem, you won't be able to remove it and you will lose time. You will need to jump off the chest of drawers run around, lose line of sight and try again.

Red Gem Collection

  1. After completing the gem puzzle, go back through the Mansion to the main hall to the mirror room.
GEM IMPORTANCE

The Red Gem is a critical item needed for another puzzle. Without it, you cannot progress to the next major section of the speedrun.

Mirror Room Access

The mirror room contains important items and serves as a transition point to the underground areas. Proper navigation here saves significant time.

Navigation Strategy

  1. Exit the Gem puzzle room and navigate to the mirror room
  2. Use the helmet key for the final time on the door.
  3. Move to the end of the hallway and to the back room with all the mirrors.
  4. Bait and dodge the zombie in the room.
  5. Grab the box, inspect it, insert the red gem and solve the puzzle.
  6. Take the emblem key from the box.
  7. Return to the pool area in the courtyard..
