About StruggleR

RACING COMPETITION • JANUARY 18, 2026

What is StruggleR?

StruggleR is an exciting racing competition where gamers race their favourite games against each other in head-to-head speedrun battles. It's part of the Wheel of Struggle community, bringing together speedrunners from all skill levels to compete, learn, and have fun.

The format is simple but thrilling: two competitors learn the same speedrun route, practice their execution, and then race live to see who can complete the game fastest. It's not just about raw skill—it's about consistency, adaptation, and handling the pressure of live competition.

The Journey to Race Day

Both racers are following this guide to learn the optimal route through the Spencer Mansion. They'll master:

  • Optimal routing through all 16 sections
  • Boss strategies for Yawn, Elder Crimson, Plant 42, Black Tiger, and Tyrant
  • Advanced techniques like pump cancelling, door cancelling, and enemy manipulation
  • Inventory management throughout the entire run
  • RNG management for dealing with unpredictable elements

The race will test not only their execution but their ability to stay calm under pressure, adapt to bad RNG, and make split-second decisions when things don't go according to plan.
