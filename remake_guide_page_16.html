Tyrant Final Boss Fight

Head to the elevator by the refueling room and go up for the final showdown with Wesker.

Combat Strategy

  1. Skip 3 cutscenes
  2. Equip Magnum (Barry's Magnum)
  3. Kill Tyrant with ONE shot
  4. Grab key from Wesker
  5. Unlock door from the console in the room and head back up to the helipad
One-Shot Kill

Barry's Magnum is a one-shot kill for Tyrant. Simply equip it, aim, and fire. The boss dies instantly, allowing you to grab the key and proceed to the escape sequence.

Helipad Escape

  1. Proceed to helipad, which is through a set of doors located at the entrance of the lab. Use the Lab Key to unlock the door.
  2. Place Fuse Unit in mechanism
  3. Go up another elevator
  4. Pick up Signal Rockets
  5. Use Signal Rockets

Now that you've completed your first run, consider:

Final Tips for Improvement

Consistency over Speed: Focus on completing runs consistently before pushing for faster times. A completed run is always better than a reset.

One Section at a Time: Don't try to optimise everything at once. Master one section, then move to the next.

Watch Your Splits: Identify your weakest sections and practise those specifically.

Stay Positive: Every run teaches you something. Even bad runs build muscle memory and experience.
