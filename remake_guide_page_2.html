Section Objectives

  • Skip opening cutscenes and obtain Lockpick from Barry
  • Collect Emblem from dining room fireplace
  • Navigate Murder Hallway and collect Arrow
  • Access graveyard and retrieve Sword Key from crypt
  • Collect essential items from East Wing (Lighter, Dog Whistle, Chemical)
  • Complete Dog Whistle encounter and obtain Dog Collar

Initial Setup

  1. Opening Cutscenes: Mash to skip through 4 opening cutscenes. Note that you cannot mash through the "survival horror" text - you must wait for it to complete.
  2. Barry Encounter: When gaining control of Jill, immediately un-equip the handgun. Move back to the main hall, skip the cutscene, and receive the Lockpick from Barry.
  3. Dining Room: Run back through the dining room.
  4. Emblem Collection: Pick up the Emblem from above the fireplace. This item is essential for later progression.
MOVEMENT optimisation

Remember that movement is faster without a weapon equipped or with the knife equipped. Un-equipping weapons during non-combat sections saves significant time over the course of a full run.

Murder Hallway 1 & Graveyard Section

Murder Hallway Navigation

  1. Run through the dining room door past Kenneth's body and up the stairs to Murder Hallway.
  2. Run past the first zombie in the upstairs hallway, then grab the Arrow.
FAST STRATEGY

Advanced: Attempt to bait the zombie past the arrow location. If the zombie is sleeping (not responsive), fall back to the safe strategy immediately.

SAFE STRATEGY

Beginner-Friendly: Wait by the arrow until you can move around the zombie against the wall on the other side. This guarantees you avoid damage but costs a few seconds.

Graveyard Access

  1. Unlock the right door with the Lockpick and run towards the Main Hall.
  2. Go down the stairs but proceed through the "secret" graveyard entrance at the top of the stairs instead of continuing down.
  3. Navigate to the Arrow gravestone, examine the arrow to remove the arrowhead, then place it into the gravestone mechanism.
  4. Proceed into the crypt to retrieve the book, pulling the Sword Key from its back.
STAIR SKATING RECOMMENDED

Stair skating up and down these stairs is highly recommended as it is a long staircase. Proper execution will save you several seconds on this segment alone.

East Wing Collection

Item Route

  1. Navigate to the eastern stairwell area.
  2. Proceed through the door past the third zombie, avoiding the next zombie.
  3. Enter the room containing the Lighter and Dog Whistle, grabbing them in that specific order for optimal inventory management.
  4. Collect the Chemical from the outside area.
SAFETY HERBS AVAILABLE

There are a few safety herbs you can grab in the outdoor area before the dogs jump over the fence. Be aware of inventory space limitations - only grab if needed and you have room.

Dog Whistle Encounter

This is one of the first combat encounters in the speedrun and requires precise execution to avoid damage and time loss.

Optimal Strategy

  1. Once outside, equip the handgun.
  2. Use the Dog Whistle to spawn the dog.
  3. Move to the upper left corner of the area.
  4. Aim at the wall and spam the "change target" button until you're aiming at the dog.
ADVANCED TECHNIQUE

Quick Shots: Use quick shots to kill the dog as close as possible. Success is hampered by health and aggression RNG, but when executed properly, this is the fastest method.

Post-Combat

  1. After killing the dog, un-equip the handgun.
  2. Grab the Dog Collar and leave the area.
  3. When prompted, drop the Dog Whistle as it's no longer needed.
FREE HEAL OPPORTUNITY

If you are on caution status going into or leaving this fight, you have access to a free heal (3 uses maximum). Make use of it if you didn't pick up a herb in the Chemical area previously.

Enemy RNG Management

Every enemy in the game has RNG affecting multiple factors:

  • Health: Some enemies take more shots to kill
  • Movement: Walking speed and lunge timing vary
  • Aggression: How quickly they respond to your presence
  • Facing Direction: Starting position affects bait opportunities

Section Summary

At this point, you should have collected:

  • Sword Key (critical for progression)
  • Lockpick (from Barry)
  • Emblem
  • Lighter
  • Dog Collar
  • Chemical
