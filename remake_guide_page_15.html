Section Objectives

  • Access computer terminal (JOHN/ADA/CELL)
  • Obtain Lab Key (code 8462)
  • Manage fuel canister safely
  • Activate power to elevator

Final Safety Save

This section has an insta-kill risk with the fuel canister. Consider saving before continuing.

Fountain to 'O' Room Route

Fountain

  • After leaving the Lisa encounter, head to the Fountain to complete the puzzle.
  • Examine Last Book Vol. 2 to obtain the Medal of Wolf; place it in the left slot.
  • Examine Last Book Vol. 1 to obtain the Medal of Eagle; place it in the right slot.
  • Skip the cutscene and take the elevator down.

Laboratory Entrance

  • Take two lefts and climb down the ladder.

Item Box Room

  • Move straight forward and through the double doors.

Laboratory Stairway

  • Dodge the zombie at the first corner, and another near (ideally on) the stairs.
  • Go through the double doors at the bottom.

'O' Room

  • Equip Barry's .44 Magnum and shoot the zombie in the archway.
  • Do not unequip it yet — it's time to go all-out.
  • Exit at the far end and go through the double doors on the left.

Computer Access

  1. Go down stairs through tunnel to computer room
  2. Enter username: JOHN
  3. Enter password: ADA
  4. Open both doors using password: CELL

Laboratory Key

  1. Go upstairs to room at top
  2. Enter code into wall console:
Lab Key Code
8462
  1. Grab Lab Key from the area that has opened behind you in the room.

Fuel Management

  1. Head to refueling room
  2. Kill the Chimera in the room.
  3. Grab Fuel Canister
  4. Head to the X-Ray room and kill the zombie in the room.
  5. Fill the canister in the X-Ray room.
  6. Head back to input slot
  7. CRITICAL PATTERN: 4 steps running, 2 steps walking (or walk the entire way). If the canister overheats and explodes, Jill dies instantly.
  8. Place canister
  9. Activate power to elevator in the next room.
