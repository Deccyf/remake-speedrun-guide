Section Objectives

  • Navigate eastern stairwell with three Hunters
  • Complete crusher puzzle room
  • Collect Last Book 1 from underground
  • Navigate kitchen and collect Battery
  • Access elevator to prepare for Yawn 2

This section introduces Hunters, the most dangerous regular enemies in the game. They can one-shot kill you with their decapitation attack, making this one of the riskiest sections of the speedrun.

Hunter Staircase Navigation

Proceed to the eastern stairwell room. This area now contains three Hunters that must be navigated carefully.

Critical Hunter Mechanics

One-Shot Kill Potential

Hunters can instantly kill you if they stop, screech, and lunge. This decapitation attack bypasses all health and results in immediate Game Over. Never let a Hunter complete its screech animation while close to you.

Optimal Navigation Strategy

  1. Move forward until the first camera transition
  2. Wait for the upstairs Hunter to run all the way to the overhang directly above Jill
  3. Wait for the Hunter to stop in position
  4. As the Hunter is about to screech, begin moving and stair skating up
Timing is Critical

The key is waiting for the Hunter to fully commit to its position above you before starting your ascent. If you move too early, the Hunter will track your movement and potentially intercept you on the stairs.

SAFETY STRATEGY

Hunter Elimination: There are going to be 3 Hunters in this stairway. If you get cornered or feel unsafe, equip your Grenade Launcher and load it with grenade rounds if you have any. Blow away any Hunters that threaten you.

This costs ammunition but guarantees survival. Better to lose 5-10 seconds than reset the entire run.

Crusher Puzzle Room

Proceed through the furthest door at the end of the hall, through the Helmet Key door, and into the crusher puzzle room.

Statue Puzzle Solution

  1. Push the statue all the way to the end of the track
  2. Run back around to the topmost corner of the room
  3. Push the switch to activate the crusher
  4. Quickly run back around to the statue
  5. Push the statue into its slot before the crusher pushes you back to the middle of the room
Timing Challenge

You have limited time after pushing the switch to return to the statue and push it into position. Run at full speed and don't hesitate. If you're too slow, the crusher pushes the statue back to the middle of the room and you'll lose alot of time going back to press the switch again.

  1. Once the puzzle is solved, proceed down the hole
  2. Pick up Last Book 1

Underground Section

Navigate through the underground area to access the elevator and continue toward Yawn 2.

Spider Navigation

  1. Go down the ladder
  2. Run past the spiders - they are slow so you should be fine dodging their spray
Blue Herb Available

If you have been poisoned earlier in the run, there is a Blue Herb through the next door. However, if you're not poisoned, skip it to save time.

Power Switch Activation

  1. Approach the zombie blocking the power switch
  2. Hug the inner wall while moving towards the zombie
  3. Bait a lunge, then move around his other side after he lunges
  4. Flip the switch to activate the elevator

Battery and Kitchen Route

Maneuver around the zombies to reach the kitchen door, then collect the Battery for later use.

Kitchen Navigation

  1. Proceed into the kitchen
  2. Hug whichever wall the zombie is closest to while moving towards him
  3. Bait a lunge, then move around his left side to the elevator
  4. Take the elevator up

Battery Collection

  1. Quickly move around the corner towards the battery room
  2. Grab the Battery
  3. Head to Murder Hall
Optional Safety Items

There is a Battery Pack on the floor (defensive item) and some Acid Shells in the battery room. Consider grabbing these if you want extra safety against Hunters, as they are now prevalent throughout the Mansion.

Murder Hall Return

Navigate back through Murder Hall one final time:

  1. The first zombie must be baited or tanked
  2. Check the furthest mirror to see if the second zombie is in front of the Crimson Head
  3. Bait the Crimson Head unless he swung at the second zombie
  4. Head through to the Armour Key puzzle area from earlier
  5. Go through the door at the end
Healing Opportunity

There is some healing available on the lead up to the Yawn 2 encounter. Once you get the cutscene for Yawn 2, you won't be able to go back until Yawn is defeated. Make sure you're at least in Fine (green) health before proceeding.

What's Next

Section 12 covers the Yawn 2 boss fight, light puzzle, Red Gem collection, and mirror room navigation. Yawn 2 requires significantly more ammunition than Yawn 1, as you'll actually fight and defeat the boss this time.

Practice Tips

Hunter Staircase: This is the highest-risk section of the entire speedrun for instant death. Practise extensively on dedicated runs. Many world-class runners have lost PB attempts here. If you're uncomfortable, use the safety strategy and eliminate Hunters with the Grenade Launcher.

Crusher Puzzle: The timing window is tight but consistent. Practise the run from switch to statue until you can execute it without panic.

Save Consideration: Some runners save before the Hunter staircase. While this costs 5-10 seconds, it provides insurance against the most dangerous section. Consider this for important runs.
