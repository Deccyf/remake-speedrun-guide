Time-Sensitive Events

Richard Timer 5 minutes real-time after speaking to Richard. Must complete Music Room, Clock, and reach Yawn.
Dog Whistle Fight Quick shots for fast kill. Free heal available if on Caution.
Neptune Skip (Advanced) Run past Neptune on specific line. Instant death if failed. Push console method is safer.

Top Tips for Success

Save Frequently: Use multiple save slots during practice to isolate difficult sections
Know Reset Conditions: Elder Crimson 4-shot, major damage taken, missed Richard timer
Inventory Management: Movement faster without weapon equipped, combine items early
RNG Acceptance: Some variance is unavoidable, focus on consistent execution
