Section Objectives

This section introduces Lisa Trevor, a recurring enemy who will appear multiple times throughout the speedrun. Learning to avoid her efficiently is critical for maintaining optimal times.

Weather Vane Puzzle

Getting to the Weather Vanes

To reach the weather vane puzzle area, follow this route from the courtyard entrance:

  1. Exit the mansion, going through the gallery puzzle gate you just went through. Through the courtyard door (requires the metal and stone object from previous section)
  2. Move down the stone steps into the lower courtyard area
  3. Follow the path that leads toward Lisa's Hut
  4. Skip the radio cutscene
  5. Look for two weather vanes one blue and one red

Puzzle Location

Move down the steps from the courtyard entrance and proceed toward Lisa's Hut. Before reaching the hut, you'll encounter two weather vanes that must be positioned correctly.

Puzzle Solution

Weather Vane Positions

Stop each vane at the same downward position relative to the screen:

  • First Vane (Red): Stop at W (West)
  • Second Vane (Blue): Stop at N (North)

Both vanes should point downward on your screen when correctly positioned.

RNG Element

The movement of these vanes is RNG-based. They spin at variable speeds and you must activate them at the correct moment. This can add 5-15 seconds of variance depending on RNG. There's no strategy to manipulate this - simply wait for the correct position and activate.

Lisa Trevor Encounter

After solving the weather vane puzzle, proceed to Lisa's Hut. This is your first encounter with Lisa Trevor, one of the game's most unique enemies.

Square Crank Collection

  1. Move to the very back of Lisa's Hut
  2. Pick up the Square Crank (essential for water management)
  3. Go to the nearby storage box
  4. Deposit the Armour Key (first item in inventory)
  5. Deposit the Lighter (second item in inventory)
Why Deposit These Items?

The Armour Key has served its purpose (you've accessed all armour key doors), and the Lighter is no longer needed. Freeing up inventory space now prevents management issues later in the Residence.

Lisa Trevor Escape Strategy

At the end of the cutscene, Lisa Trevor spawns and will attempt to attack you. This encounter requires precise timing to avoid damage.

Critical Timing

Wait approximately half a second after the cutscene ends before moving. Then run past Lisa on her right side. This timing avoids damage every time.

Running too soon will almost always result in Lisa hitting you. The half-second wait is crucial for her AI to enter the correct state.

Lisa Trevor Mechanics

Lisa Trevor is unique among enemies in Resident Evil:

  • Cannot be killed: All damage is temporary, she will always recover
  • High damage output: Her attacks deal significant damage
  • Recurring enemy: You'll encounter her again in the underground sections
  • Slow movement: Can be outrun with proper routing
  • Scripted spawns: She spawns at predictable locations

Courtyard Water Management

After escaping Lisa's Hut, proceed back through the courtyard with your newly acquired Square Crank.

Storage Room Navigation

  1. Proceed back down the path away from the hut
  2. Avoid the zombie after the stairs (bait or run past)
  3. Return to the storage room at the Courtyard entrance
  4. Grab the FAS (First Aid Spray) from the room
  5. Proceed around the room through the double doors toward Residence area
First Aid Spray

This FAS provides a full heal and should be saved for emergencies. Only use it if you're in Danger status and don't have time for herbs. It's particularly valuable for the upcoming Residence section where damage is common.

Water Lowering Sequence

  1. Run past the dogs through the gate and up the steps
  2. Use the Square Crank on the mechanism to lower the water
  3. Proceed around the path to the elevator
  4. Take the elevator down
Dog Management

The dogs in this area can be aggressive. Run straight through without stopping. If you maintain forward momentum, they typically won't catch you. Stopping or hesitating increases the chance of taking damage.

Residence Access

After descending in the elevator, navigate through the gate and along the winding path into the Residence area.

Path Navigation

The path from the elevator to the Residence is straightforward but has a few enemies:

  • Avoid engaging dogs by the waterfall and enter the opposite gate.
  • Run forward continuously. The falling snakes will not get a chance to bite you if you just run forward.
  • Stay on the main path
  • Run through the final door to enter the Residence proper

Current Inventory Check

Before entering the Residence, verify your inventory contains:

  • Grenade Launcher
  • Square Crank (just collected)
  • FAS (first aid spray)
  • Grenade Launcher rounds
  • Empty slots for upcoming items
What's Next

Section 8 covers the early Residence section including Room 002, Room 001, bookshelf puzzle, Aqua Ring access, and the challenging shark room encounter with Neptune. The Residence section is item-heavy, so inventory management becomes critical.

Practice Tips for This Section

Weather Vane RNG: Don't get frustrated by bad RNG here. The variance is unavoidable and 10-15 seconds lost here can easily be made up in other sections with cleaner execution.

Lisa Trevor Timing: Practise the half-second wait after the cutscene. This is one of the most consistent damage-avoidance strats in the run. Missing it costs both health and time to heal later.

Square Crank Importance: The Square Crank is used multiple times in the run. Don't deposit it in storage - you'll need it for the pool later in the route.

Alternate Strategies

Safety Herbs: If you're concerned about damage during the Residence section, there are green herbs available before entering. However, this costs inventory space and time - only grab if you're in Caution or Danger.

Dog Avoidance: Some runners prefer using the handgun to kill one dog near the water mechanism. This costs ammo and time but guarantees safety. For optimal times, run past without fighting.
