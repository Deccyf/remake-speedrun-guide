Elder Crimson Boss Fight

Section Objectives

  • Navigate to crypt and place all four Death Masks
  • Prepare incendiary shells and Grenade Launcher
  • Defeat Elder Crimson with optimal shot count
  • Collect Stone and Metal Object from coffin
  • Exit through graveyard and gallery to Courtyard

The Elder Crimson is one of the most RNG-dependent boss fights in the speedrun. Shot count varies from 3 to 4 shells, and the boss can fall down mid-fight, adding additional time.

Death Mask Placement

Exit out the nearby gate into the graveyard and proceed down to the crypt to begin placing the masks. The masks must be placed in a specific order from left to right.

Mask Placement Order (Left to Right)

1
Without Mouth - Speak No Evil
2
Without Eyes - See No Evil
3
Without Nose - (Breaks the Three Wise Monkeys pattern)
4
Complete Mask - Without Mouth, Eyes, and Nose
PREPARE BEFORE PLACING FINAL MASK

Before placing the fifth mask position, you must:

  • Combine incendiary shells into the Grenade Launcher
  • Equip the Grenade Launcher
  • Ensure you have at least 3 incendiary shells loaded

Once you place the final mask and approach the coffin, the cutscene triggers immediately and combat begins.

Combat Strategy

After you regain control of Jill following the cutscene, immediately execute the following strategy:

Optimal Positioning

  1. Immediately close the gap between Jill and the Elder Crimson
  2. Move approximately halfway to the boss before stopping
  3. Begin firing incendiary shells
WHY CLOSE RANGE?

Firing from closer range increases accuracy and ensures all shots connect. The Elder Crimson has a large hitbox, but firing from maximum distance can result in missed shots, especially if it's moving.

RNG Outcomes

The Elder Crimson fight has three possible RNG outcomes that affect your time:

Possible Outcomes

Best RNG: Elder Crimson dies in 3 shots without falling down. This is the ideal outcome and saves the most time. Simply fire 3 incendiary shells in rapid succession using pump cancelling.
Medium RNG: Elder Crimson falls down after 1 or 2 shots. When this happens, you must run up close to the fallen boss and aim down at it to shoot again and finish it off. This adds approximately 3-5 seconds to the fight.
Worst RNG: Elder Crimson takes 4 shots to kill. In a high-level run, this is considered a reset. However, for casual runs, this can be mitigated by switching back to regular grenade rounds if you have them available.
RESET CONSIDERATION

If you're going for a competitive time and Elder Crimson requires 4 shots, many runners choose to reset the run here. However, for practice runs and casual attempts, continue through to build consistency and muscle memory.

Item Collection and Exit

Post-Combat Actions

  1. After Elder Crimson is defeated, un-equip the Grenade Launcher immediately
  2. Approach the coffin
  3. Retrieve the Stone and Metal Object from inside
  4. Leave through the graveyard exit
