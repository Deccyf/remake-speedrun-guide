Armour Key Sequence

This section introduces one of the most dangerous areas in the speedrun: Murder Hall with an activated Crimson Head. Proper execution is essential to avoid significant damage.

Coin Puzzle Setup

  1. Pick up the Armour Key from the designated room.
  2. Open the Dog Collar in your inventory.
  3. Spin around the "coin" item (examine and rotate it).
  4. Activate its back side and place it into the pedestal.
INVENTORY MANAGEMENT

The coin puzzle requires careful inventory manipulation. Practise the spin and activation sequence to minimise menu time.

Murder Hall Navigation (Crimson Head Active)

This is arguably the most dangerous section of the early game. The Crimson Head is extremely aggressive and can deal significant damage if not handled properly.

SAFETY ROUTE

Recommended for Beginners: Run through the first doorway directly in front of the Crimson Head. The Crimson will always lunge and fail when you use this route, giving you time to exit early and avoid the remaining zombies in the hallway.

Navigation to Grenade Launcher

  1. After exiting Murder Hall safely, go back through the door you went through after getting the arrow.
  2. Proceed through the top of the main hall to the door at the top right of where you are facing.
  3. Travel around the southern walkway to the Armour Key door leading to the Grenade Launcher area.

Grenade Launcher Access

The Grenade Launcher is one of the most important weapons in the Any% speedrun. It's used for multiple boss fights and is essential for optimal times.

Forrest Encounter

  1. Speak to Barry in the cutscene.
  2. Mourn Forrest (the cutscene will play automatically).
  3. Receive the Grenade Launcher - this large, powerful weapon will be your primary boss-killing tool.
  4. Never return to this location (it's no longer needed).

Essential Item Acquired

The Grenade Launcher is critical for multiple boss encounters including:

  • Elder Crimson (3-4 incendiary shells)
  • Black Tiger (web removal + damage)
  • Plant 42 (3 incendiary shells)
  • Yawn 2 (4-5 grenade rounds)

Richard Encounter and Timer

Saving Richard

  1. Return to the main hall.
  2. Go into the East Wing and speak to Richard.
  3. Skip the cutscene and leave immediately.
TIME-SENSITIVE EVENT

Richard has 5 minutes in real-time before he dies. All upcoming tasks (Serum collection, Death Mask 1, Music Room, Clock Puzzle, and reaching Yawn) must be completed within 5 minutes. This is tight but very doable with proper execution.

The timer begins the moment you trigger Richard's cutscene. If you take longer than 5 minutes, Richard will die before you can save him, and you'll miss out on a critical storyline trigger that affects your route timing.

Serum Save Room

Item Management

  1. Proceed to the Serum save room in the West Wing.
  2. Deposit the following items in the item box:
    • Handgun (no longer needed for combat)
    • Knife (taking up inventory space)
    • Sword Key (used all doors requiring it)
  3. Before leaving: Pick up the Serum from the room.
SERUM FOR RICHARD

This Serum is to heal Richard. Be careful in Yawn fight asYawn poison will end your run Poison from Yawn is lethal - you will slowly lose health until you die if not treated. If you do get posioned you must return to get the serum to heal yourself.

Death Mask 1 Collection

This begins the Death Mask collection sequence that will span multiple sections. You need all four masks to access the Elder Crimson boss fight.

Water Tank Puzzle

  1. Move to the water tank room.
  2. Use the Chemical on the water tank.
  3. Examine the tank, selecting the first options in the dialogue tree until the cutscene begins.
  4. Proceed to pick up Death Mask 1 and exit the room.

Current Inventory Status

At this point, your inventory should contain:

  • Grenade Launcher (equipped or in first slot)
  • Emblem
  • Lighter
  • Serum (to heal Richard)
  • Death Mask 1
